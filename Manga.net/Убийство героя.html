Title: Kill the hero


Description:

This world was not merciful, and I responded with the same sincere hatred. When it became the Game, nothing changed for me. However, meeting Li Se-Chung - a hero, a Warrior, a member of the most revered of the guilds - gave me a home, comrades and the meaning of life. The point was to destroy those damn dungeons and end the Game! I died from a sword in the back. From the sword of the one whom I believed, at the gates of the last dungeon ... Now I have returned and I know exactly who will be the prey of the "hunting dog" Kim Woo Jin, no matter what it takes!
